## <a name="phnompenh"></a> Phnom Penh

### Getting there
* Bus: from HCMC/Saigon - [Mekong Express](http://catmekongexpress.com/schedule.html)
* Bus: Air conditioned express bus, ~7hrs
* Air: Vietnam Air, ~45min, $188 USD

* Ta Prohm
* Bayon
